If you have already signed up as a participant with the ITU Global Cyber Drill Office, and they have already confirmed you, you will be able to complete your registration and access the ITU 2020 Global Cyber Drill platform on this page.

The exercises are open only for National/Governmental CIRTs/ CSIRTs and each registered country will be represented by one team consisting two (2) to four (4) participants.

Only ITU pre-authorized participants are advised to fill in the next
registration form.

Your data you submit next will be checked with the ITU team and, if successful, you will receive a confirmation by e-mail together with the instructions on how to access and use CYBER RANGES and prepare for ITU 2020 Global Cyber Drill.

The next-generation CYBER RANGES by Silensec provides the ITU 2020 Global Cyber Drill with:

  • A large simulated infrastructure
  • Support for realistic simulation environments
  • Orchestration
  • Great Cyber Attack Simulation
  • Scenario Creation and Collaboration
  • Tracking of individual users and teams' activities
